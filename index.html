About Me

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical & Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania, working with Prof. Christos Davatzikos. I completed bachelors and masters in electrical engineering at Indian Institue of Technology, Delhi . You can see a current list of my papers here. I enjoy cycling and inline skating (see my strava), backcountry skiing, traveling and painting (see my instagram ) in my spare time.
